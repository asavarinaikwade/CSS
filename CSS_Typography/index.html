The Ketan Agrawal murder case has taken a forensic turn, with police conducting gait analysis of accused Chetan Chaudhary and recreating the crime scene at Lohagad Fort. Agrawal, a 26‑year‑old Pune realtor, allegedly died after being pushed from the fort by his fiancée Siya Goyal and her lover Chaudhary on June 18, 2026.

The Ketan Agrawal case is now at a critical stage, with forensic gait analysis, crime scene reconstructions, and mounting public pressure for harsh punishment. The next court hearing on July 3, 2026 will be pivotal in determining the direction of the trial.
